Founded in 1994, Muthayammal College of Arts & Science has emerged as an institution of fine learning. The basic tenets of education that govern the college are embodied in the words "education for all".

In the years since its inception, hundreds of rural students have been able to explore better career options and achieve high degrees of success.

The college is located on a sprawling campus that makes for a quiet learning environment. Approved by AICTE, New Delhi & Affiliated to Periyar University, Salem, Accredited by NAAC with 'A' grade, approved by DBT for STAR college Scheme, Recognized by UGC Under Section 2(f) & 12(B) it is a self financing co-educational institution and offers a total of 17 Undergraduate, 13 Post Graduate, 8-M.Phil and 5 Ph.D Research Programmes in Various disciplines.

Twenty five years in education. A million ways of creating new future.

Care is taken to ensure that infrastructure facilities compare to the best in the industry. A proactive management and a team of highly dedicated faculty ensure that learning at Muthayammal Institutions is a difference.
