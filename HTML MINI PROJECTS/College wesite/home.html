About

In addition to the numerous courses offered at the Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Research levels, in the Muthayammal College of Arts and Science the year 2010 saw the establishment of the Muthayammal Polytechnic Institution. The Institution designed exclusively to encourage vocational and job oriented training in technical subjects. The Institution has separate infrastructural and host of other amenities to make it a state of the art learning institution. The Muthayammal polytechnic institution moves ahead, with a vision to make learning accessible to every person who seeks it.
